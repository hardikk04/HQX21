The HQX

The new form of working

At a time when rapidly advancing technologies are paving the way for new forms of work, the trend back to urban production facilities is progressing. This can be observed particularly in the vibrant economic metropolis of Vienna, where the renaissance of local manufacturing areas is actively promoted. In the Austrian capital, however, space is precious, especially when it comes to creating new production environments.

A space, many uses

The HQX in Floridsdorf closes this gap and is more than just an ordinary workspace: it is a place where urban production, sales and the office merge harmoniously under one roof. On the approximately 1.9 hectare site at Shuttleworthstraße 31-33, the three-part building ensemble represents a new type of mixed use of flexible commercial space in an urban location. Perfect for companies that want to combine all of their business activities in one place.
